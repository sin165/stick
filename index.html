Stick

The most stubborn character of “The Stormlight Archive”

Facts about Stick

  • Stick is a character from “The Stormlight Archive” by Brandon Sanderson.
  • Stick is a stick.
  • Stick could be fire but it is a stick.
  • Stick lives on the planet Roshar.
  • Stick is a particularly self-aware stick.
  • Stick is very stubborn.
  • Stick is not very eloquent.
  • Shallan (another character from the same series) tried to turn Stick into fire but failed.
  • On April 1st, 2014 Brandon Sanderson released a deleted interlude from Stick's perspective.
  • You can buy the official Stick T-shirt.
